# 🐾 TomGrow: Virtual Pet & Life Tracker
> A professional companion app for habit tracking, fitness, and long-distance connection.

## 🌟 Project Overview
This app integrates a **3D Interactive Talking Tom** with daily productivity tools. It is designed to help users grow alongside their digital pet.

---

## 🛠 Features Breakdown

### 1. The Interactive Pet (3D Kitty)
* **Hallway:** Idle state where Tom stands.
* **Dining Room:** Tom sits at a table; clicking food triggers a **4-second eating animation**.
* **Bedroom:** Interactive bed that triggers a sleep state.
* **Custom Textures:** Uses high-fidelity maps for Face, Body, and Eyes.

### 2. Productivity & Fitness
* **Habit Tracker:** Daily, Weekly, and Monthly analysis with graph visualization.
* **Run Tracker:** Track distance and view progress milestones.

### 3. Love Space (LDR Sync)
* **Pairing:** Connect with a partner via unique security codes.
* **Shared Achievements:** Automatically share habit completions with your partner.
* **Media Chat:** Instagram-style interface for messaging and photos.
